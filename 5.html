The MIT Morningside Academy for Design Inaugural Conference, The Power of Design, highlights the many ways in which interdisciplinary design collaboration helps us to address our biggest societal challenges.

The Conference is structured in three Sessions:

  1. Innovating Through Design
  2. Educating in Design
  3. Scaling Design

These represent the three pillars of MAD’s activities: Research, Education, and Entrepreneurship. In each Session, global design leaders will succinctly summarize their vision for the power of design.

Each of our contributors will give a dynamic 8-minute presentation accompanied by visuals. We will benefit from the new high resolution screen of the MIT Museum Exchange Room. The contributors represent different fields of knowledge and different types of design practices. They show how design is crucial for a rich variety of disciplines–from humanities to STEM.

The event will be housed at the new MIT Museum, which will allow the general public to attend. The sessions will be public and will stream live online. The Power of Design will close with a reception to present the inaugural cohort of MAD fellows.
